Introduction


Welcome to Musical Genre Guessing!

Musical Genre Guessing is a website where the main attraction is a quiz that tries to guess one's preferred music genre out of six: Pop, Jazz, Rock, EDM, Classical, and Folk. The quiz asks different questions about the genre, while trying to guess your ideal one based on the way you answer.

There are no right or wrong answers. Every genre is a beautiful genre. Just answer the questions that you think fit your music taste the best! Best of luck and have fun~!

Keep in mind that this quiz is likely very inaccurate. There is no way a genre can be guessed with only 15 questions. This is only made for fun and as a school project. Enjoy!
