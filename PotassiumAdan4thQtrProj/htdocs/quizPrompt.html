Quiz Time!


Are you ready?

Remember, there are no right or wrong answers. Just pick the option that best describes your genre. There is no guarantee - there is a pretty high chance that we might get it wrong, but it's all in good fun.

Clicking the button below the webpage will start the quiz. There will be a reset button to reset your answers. Press the submit button if you're done. After that, the genre we think you're trying to describe will be displayed on the screen. There will be a link on the screen to go to a page with information about the genre the website guessed, prepared by me. References are in the header of the website at any time.

Remember, the website can only pick from six different genres:

  • Pop
  • Jazz
  • Rock
  • EDM
  • Classical
  • Folk

Other than that, all that's left to do is to start the quiz. Have fun!
